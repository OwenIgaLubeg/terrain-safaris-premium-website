4-Day Kidepo Valley Wildlife Safari

Untouched Wilderness, Rare Wildlife & Scenic Savannahs Explore the remote and pristine Kidepo Valley National Park, one of Uganda's most spectacular wildlife destinations.

Safari Overview

Remote Wilderness Adventure

This 4-day safari adventure is ideal for travelers seeking off the beaten path experiences. Experience pristine wilderness and dramatic landscapes, observe rare and endangered wildlife including lions, cheetahs, elephants, and leopards, visit local Karamojong communities to understand traditional lifestyles, enjoy scenic game drives in open savannah plains, and birdwatching opportunities with over 470 recorded species.

Detailed Itinerary

Your 4-Day Kidepo Adventure

Day 1

Arrival at Entebbe Airport and Transfer to Kidepo

Arrive at Entebbe International Airport and meet your professional safari guide. Fly or drive to Kidepo Valley National Park. Check-in at your safari lodge and enjoy lunch with panoramic views of the savannah. Afternoon relaxation followed by a sunset game drive across the park, spotting elephants, giraffes, and antelope herds.

Dinner and overnight at Apoka Safari Lodge or similar.
Day 2

Full day Game Drive in Kidepo

Early morning game drive in search of predators like lions and leopards and large herbivores including buffaloes and elephants. Explore the park's iconic Ntika Valley and rolling plains for photography and wildlife observation. Mid-day rest and lunch at the lodge. Afternoon game drive focusing on birdwatching, with opportunities to see ostriches, vultures, and exotic savannah species.

Evening relaxation at the lodge and overnight stay.
Day 3

Cultural Encounter & Wildlife Exploration

Morning visit to a nearby Karamojong village, meet locals, learn traditional customs, and experience cultural performances. Return to the park for a guided safari drive, exploring lesser visited areas of Kidepo Valley and photographing scenic landscapes. Optional walking safari in selected zones with expert guide explanations on flora, fauna, and tracking techniques. Sunset viewing from hilltops overlooking the savannah plains.

Dinner and overnight at the lodge.
Day 4

Final Game Drive and Return to Entebbe

Early morning game drive to catch wildlife in active hours. Breakfast at the lodge and check-out. Transfer back to Entebbe via flight or road, enjoying scenic views of northern Uganda. Arrive at Entebbe, concluding the safari.

End of Safari.
Preparation

Things to Pack for Kidepo Safari

Comfortable walking shoes and hiking boots
Wide-brim hat and sunglasses
Long-sleeved shirts and light trousers for sun protection
Light jacket for cool mornings and evenings
Binoculars and camera for wildlife photography
Insect repellent and sunscreen
Reusable water bottle and snacks
Small backpack for daily essentials
