Kenya Safari Packages

Big Five Safaris, Great Migration, Beach Holidays & Luxury Lodges - Discover diverse landscapes, iconic wildlife, rich Maasai culture, and world-class safari lodges across Kenya's most celebrated destinations.

Safari Highlights

The Ultimate African Wildlife Adventure

Kenya safari packages offer the ultimate African wildlife adventure — from witnessing the Great Migration in the Maasai Mara to relaxing on white-sand beaches along the Indian Ocean. Highlights include Big Five game drives in Maasai Mara National Reserve, witnessing the Great Wildebeest Migration (seasonal), Amboseli safaris with Mount Kilimanjaro views, flamingos & lake safaris in Lake Nakuru National Park, beach holidays in Diani Beach, luxury safari lodges and tented camps, and cultural visits with Maasai communities.

Detailed Itinerary

Your 7-Day Kenya Adventure

Day 1

Arrival in Nairobi

Arrive at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport, where your professional safari guide welcomes you. Transfer to your hotel in Nairobi. Depending on arrival time, enjoy an optional city excursion such as the Giraffe Centre or local markets.

Overnight in Nairobi.
Day 2

Transfer to Maasai Mara National Reserve

After breakfast, depart for the world-famous Maasai Mara. Enjoy scenic views of the Great Rift Valley en route. Arrive in time for lunch at your luxury lodge or tented camp. Afternoon game drive searching for lions, elephants, leopards, buffaloes, rhinos, cheetahs, and vast herds of herbivores.

Dinner and overnight in Maasai Mara.
Day 3

Full-Day Game Drive in Maasai Mara

Spend a full day exploring the reserve with morning and afternoon game drives. During migration season (July–October), witness thousands of wildebeest and zebras crossing the Mara River — one of the most spectacular wildlife events on earth. Optional hot air balloon safari at sunrise for breathtaking aerial views.

Overnight in Maasai Mara.
Day 4

Transfer to Lake Nakuru National Park

Drive to Lake Nakuru National Park, famous for its flamingos and rhino sanctuary. Enjoy an afternoon game drive spotting white and black rhinos, Rothschild giraffes, lions, and abundant birdlife along the lake shores.

Overnight in Lake Nakuru area.
Day 5

Amboseli National Park Safari

Transfer to Amboseli National Park, known for its large elephant herds and stunning views of Mount Kilimanjaro. Afternoon game drive with incredible photography opportunities of elephants against Africa's highest peak.

Dinner and overnight in Amboseli.
Day 6

Fly to the Kenyan Coast

Transfer back to Nairobi for a domestic flight to Kenya's coast. Arrive in Diani Beach for relaxation along the turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean. Enjoy luxury beach resorts, water sports, snorkeling, and Swahili cuisine.

Overnight at beach resort.
Day 7

Beach Relaxation & Departure

Enjoy a relaxed morning at the beach before transfer to the airport for your international departure.

End of Kenya Safari Package.
