16 Days Rwenzori Hiking Safari

Conquer Africa's Legendary "Mountains of the Moon" Embark on an extraordinary 16-day Rwenzori hiking expedition in the breathtaking Rwenzori Mountains National Park.

Safari Overview

High Altitude Mountaineering Adventure

This guided mountaineering adventure is ideal for trekkers seeking a challenging, scenic, and rewarding high altitude experience in Uganda. Summit Margherita Peak (5,109m) on Mount Stanley, trek through tropical rainforest, bamboo zones, and alpine moorlands, experience unique Afro-alpine vegetation and glacial scenery, and spot wildlife including Rwenzori colobus monkeys and endemic bird species.

Detailed Itinerary

Your 16-Day Hiking Expedition

Day 1

Arrival at Entebbe & Transfer to Kasese

Arrive at Entebbe International Airport, where you are welcomed by your professional safari representative. Transfer by road or domestic flight to Kasese in western Uganda, enjoying scenic countryside views, tea plantations, and crater lakes. Check into your lodge at the foothills of the Rwenzori Mountains for relaxation and trek briefing.

Overnight in Kasese.
Day 2

Trek Briefing & Acclimatization Walk

After breakfast, transfer to the park headquarters for registration and a detailed mountaineering briefing. Meet your guides, porters, and trekking team. Enjoy a short acclimatization hike through montane forest trails to prepare your body for altitude gain.

Overnight at the base lodge or first mountain camp.
Day 3

Trek to Sine Hut (2,596m)

Begin your Rwenzori trekking adventure through dense tropical rainforest filled with giant ferns, waterfalls, and unique birdlife. Watch for blue monkeys and colobus monkeys. Arrive at Sine Hut for dinner and overnight.

Overnight at Sine Hut.
Day 4

Trek to Mutinda Camp (3,582m)

Ascend through bamboo forest into the Heather Rapanea zone. The landscape transforms dramatically with moss covered trees and giant lobelias.

Overnight at Mutinda Camp.
Day 5

Acclimatization at Mutinda

Take a guided acclimatization hike to higher ridges, offering panoramic views of the Rwenzori ranges and surrounding valleys. Return to camp for rest and hydration.

Overnight at Mutinda Camp.
Day 6

Trek to Bugata Camp (4,062m)

Cross high alpine valleys and boggy terrain characterized by giant groundsels and lobelias. This section showcases the surreal Afro-alpine ecosystem.

Overnight at Bugata Camp.
Day 7

Trek to Hunwick's Camp (3,974m)

Traverse scenic ridges with spectacular views of Mount Stanley, Mount Baker, and Mount Speke. Descend slightly for overnight rest at Hunwick's Camp.

Overnight at Hunwick's Camp.
Day 8

Trek to Margherita Camp (4,485m)

Ascend toward Margherita Camp near the base of Mount Stanley. The terrain becomes rocky and glacial, offering dramatic high altitude scenery. Prepare for summit attempt.

Overnight at Margherita Camp.
Day 9

Summit Margherita Peak (5,109m)

Begin pre-dawn ascent to Margherita Peak, the highest point in the Rwenzori Mountains. Cross glaciers and rocky ridges with technical guidance. Reach the summit for breathtaking sunrise views over Uganda and Congo. Descend to Hunwick's Camp for overnight.

Overnight at Hunwick's Camp.
Day 10

Descend to Kiharo Camp

Descend through alpine landscapes and moorland vegetation, enjoying improved oxygen levels and scenic views.

Overnight at Kiharo Camp.
Day 11

Trek to Nyabitaba Hut

Continue descent through bamboo forest zones and lush rainforest trails.

Overnight at Nyabitaba Hut.
Day 12

Exit the Park & Transfer to Lodge

Complete your Rwenzori hiking expedition and transfer back to your lodge in Kasese for relaxation and hot showers.

Overnight in Kasese.
Day 13

Optional Wildlife Safari in Queen Elizabeth National Park

Enjoy an optional game drive in Queen Elizabeth National Park, spotting elephants, lions, buffaloes, and hippos.

Overnight near the park.
Day 14

Boat Cruise on Kazinga Channel

Experience a scenic boat safari along the Kazinga Channel, famous for dense hippo populations and abundant birdlife.

Overnight near the park.
Day 15

Return to Entebbe

Scenic transfer back to Entebbe with stopovers for photography and souvenir shopping.

Overnight in Entebbe.
Day 16

Departure

Transfer to Entebbe International Airport for your outbound flight.

End of Safari.
