12 Days Private Uganda Luxury Safari

Exclusive Uganda Gorilla Trekking, Wildlife & Scenic Landscapes Experience An exceptional 12-day private Uganda safari designed for discerning travelers seeking luxury lodges and personalized service.

Overview

Immersive Luxury Safari Journey

This 12-day private Uganda luxury safari offers an immersive journey through Uganda's most iconic national parks and scenic destinations. Experience thrilling game drives in Murchison Falls National Park, chimpanzee tracking in Kibale Forest National Park, classic savannah safaris in Queen Elizabeth National Park, and life changing gorilla trekking in Bwindi Impenetrable National Park. This itinerary combines luxury safari lodges, breathtaking landscapes, cultural encounters, and personalized guiding for a truly exclusive African safari experience.

Detailed Itinerary

Your 12-Day Luxury Adventure

Day 1

Arrival at Entebbe International Airport

Upon arrival at Entebbe International Airport, you will be welcomed by your private professional safari guide. Transfer to a luxury boutique hotel in Entebbe overlooking Lake Victoria. Enjoy a detailed safari briefing and relax after your international flight. Depending on arrival time, optional sunset views along Lake Victoria or a visit to Entebbe Botanical Gardens can be arranged.

Overnight in Entebbe.
Day 2

Transfer to Murchison Falls National Park

After breakfast, begin your private scenic drive north toward Murchison Falls National Park. En route, stop at Ziwa Rhino Sanctuary for guided rhino tracking on foot, offering a rare opportunity to see white rhinos in their natural habitat. Proceed to the park and visit the Top of the Falls, where the River Nile dramatically forces itself through a narrow gorge, creating one of Africa's most powerful waterfalls a spectacular photographic highlight.

Overnight at a luxury safari lodge inside the park.
Day 3

Sunrise Game Drive & Nile Luxury Boat Safari

Start your morning with a private sunrise game drive across the vast savannah plains. Search for lions, leopards, elephants, giraffes, buffaloes, hartebeests, and antelope species while enjoying personalized wildlife interpretation from your expert guide. In the afternoon, enjoy a boat safari cruise along the Nile to the base of Murchison Falls. Witness large pods of hippos, Nile crocodiles, elephants bathing along the riverbanks, and diverse birdlife in this biodiverse ecosystem. Optional sundowner experience at your lodge.

Overnight in Murchison Falls.
Day 4

Transfer to Kibale Forest National Park

After breakfast, embark on a scenic drive south through rural Uganda, passing rolling hills, tea plantations, and crater lakes toward Kibale Forest. Arrive at your luxury forest lodge nestled near the rainforest. Spend the afternoon relaxing or enjoying guided nature walks around the lodge area.

Overnight near Kibale Forest.
Day 5

Chimpanzee Tracking & Bigodi Cultural Experience

Enjoy early breakfast before heading into Kibale Forest for chimpanzee tracking. Walk through lush tropical rainforest in search of habituated chimpanzee communities. Observe their feeding habits, social structures, and playful interactions a thrilling primate safari experience. In the afternoon, visit Bigodi Wetland Sanctuary for birdwatching and cultural immersion. Meet local community members and learn about traditional livelihoods and conservation initiatives.

Overnight in Kibale.
Day 6

Transfer to Queen Elizabeth National Park

Drive to Queen Elizabeth National Park, known for its diverse ecosystems of savannah plains, wetlands, and crater lakes. Arrive in time for an evening private game drive in the Kasenyi plains. Expect sightings of elephants, buffaloes, lions, hyenas, and various antelope species against stunning sunset backdrops.

Overnight in a luxury safari lodge.
Day 7

Game Drive & Kazinga Channel Boat Cruise

Begin the day with an early morning game drive to maximize wildlife viewing opportunities. Depending on routing, visit the Ishasha sector famous for tree-climbing lions. In the afternoon, enjoy a scenic boat cruise along the Kazinga Channel, home to one of Africa's largest concentrations of hippos and abundant birdlife including pelicans, African fish eagles, and cormorants.

Overnight in Queen Elizabeth National Park.
Day 8

Transfer to Bwindi Impenetrable National Park

Journey through the scenic Kigezi Highlands to Bwindi Impenetrable National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and home to nearly half of the world's remaining mountain gorillas. Arrive at your luxury forest lodge with panoramic views of the mist covered rainforest.

Overnight in Bwindi.
Day 9

Mountain Gorilla Trekking Experience

After briefing by Uganda Wildlife Authority rangers, begin your unforgettable mountain gorilla trekking adventure. Trek through dense rainforest terrain guided by expert trackers until you locate a habituated gorilla family. Spend one extraordinary hour observing these gentle giants watching silverbacks assert dominance, mothers care for infants, and juveniles play. This intimate wildlife encounter is widely regarded as one of Africa's most powerful safari experiences. Return to the lodge for relaxation and optional spa treatments.

Overnight in Bwindi.
Day 10

Transfer to Lake Bunyonyi (Relaxation Day)

After breakfast, transfer to the serene Lake Bunyonyi, known as one of Africa's most beautiful lakes. Surrounded by terraced hills and dotted with small islands, Lake Bunyonyi offers peaceful relaxation after gorilla trekking. Optional canoeing, boat rides, swimming, or cultural visits to local communities.

Overnight at a luxury lakeside lodge.
Day 11

Return to Entebbe (Equator Stop)

Drive back to Entebbe with a stop at the Uganda Equator for photography and souvenir shopping. Enjoy lunch en route before arriving in Entebbe. Farewell dinner at your hotel overlooking Lake Victoria.

Overnight in Entebbe.
Day 12

Departure

Transfer to Entebbe International Airport for your departure flight, concluding your exclusive 12-day private Uganda luxury safari.

End of safari.
