9 Days Uganda Gorilla & Wildlife Safari

Ultimate Uganda Gorilla Trekking & Big Five Wildlife Experience Combine world-class mountain gorilla trekking in Bwindi with thrilling African wildlife safaris.

Overview

Premium Primate & Savannah Adventure

This 9-day Uganda gorilla and wildlife safari is a premium primate and savannah adventure designed for travelers seeking authentic African safari experiences. From the thunderous waterfalls of Murchison Falls National Park to chimpanzee tracking in Kibale Forest National Park, classic game drives in Queen Elizabeth National Park, and life changing gorilla trekking in Bwindi Impenetrable National Park, this itinerary delivers unmatched biodiversity, scenic landscapes, and unforgettable wildlife encounters in the Pearl of Africa.

Detailed Itinerary

Your 9-Day Adventure

Day 1

Arrival at Entebbe International Airport

Upon arrival at Entebbe International Airport, you will be warmly welcomed by your professional English-speaking safari guide. Transfer to your comfortable hotel in Entebbe for relaxation after your international flight. Enjoy a detailed safari briefing covering gorilla trekking guidelines, wildlife viewing expectations, and conservation insights about Uganda's national parks. Depending on arrival time, optional activities include a visit to the Entebbe Botanical Gardens or a sunset walk along Lake Victoria shores.

Overnight in Entebbe.
Day 2

Transfer to Murchison Falls National Park & Rhino Tracking

After early breakfast, embark on a scenic drive to Murchison Falls National Park, Uganda's largest and oldest conservation area. En route, stop at Ziwa Rhino Sanctuary for an exciting guided white rhino tracking experience on foot completing your opportunity to see the "Big Five" in Uganda. Continue north through rural landscapes and local villages toward Murchison Falls. Upon arrival, visit the Top of the Falls, where the River Nile explodes through a narrow 7-meter gorge, creating one of Africa's most powerful waterfalls a breathtaking photography moment.

Overnight in Murchison Falls National Park.
Day 3

Sunrise Game Drive & Nile Boat Cruise

Start your day with an early morning game drive across the vast savannah plains of Murchison Falls. Expect sightings of African elephants, Rothschild giraffes, lions, leopards, Cape buffaloes, Uganda kobs, hartebeests, and hyenas. Your expert guide enhances the experience with in-depth wildlife interpretation and tracking skills. In the afternoon, enjoy a boat safari cruise along the River Nile to the base of Murchison Falls. This boat trip offers exceptional close range views of hippos, Nile crocodiles, African fish eagles, kingfishers, and other water birds. The dramatic waterfall backdrop creates a spectacular safari highlight.

Overnight in Murchison Falls National Park.
Day 4

Transfer to Kibale Forest National Park

After breakfast, drive south toward Kibale Forest National Park, famously known as the "Primate Capital of the World." The journey offers scenic views of tea plantations, crater lakes, and the foothills of the Rwenzori Mountains. Arrive in the afternoon and relax at your lodge surrounded by lush tropical rainforest. Prepare for the next day's chimpanzee trekking adventure.

Overnight near Kibale Forest.
Day 5

Chimpanzee Trekking & Bigodi Wetland Experience

Early morning briefing at Kibale headquarters before entering the forest for chimpanzee tracking. Kibale is home to over 1,500 chimpanzees and 12 other primate species including red colobus monkeys and L'Hoest's monkeys. Track a habituated chimpanzee community through dense rainforest, observing their social behaviors, vocal communication, feeding patterns, and playful interactions a thrilling and educational primate encounter. In the afternoon, visit Bigodi Wetland Sanctuary for a guided nature walk. This community run eco-tourism project offers excellent birdwatching opportunities and insights into local culture and conservation initiatives.

Overnight in Kibale.
Day 6

Transfer to Queen Elizabeth National Park

After breakfast, drive to Queen Elizabeth National Park, Uganda's most popular savannah wildlife destination. The park boasts diverse ecosystems including open grasslands, crater lakes, wetlands, and the famous Kazinga Channel. Arrive in time for an evening game drive across the Kasenyi plains. Search for lions, elephants, buffaloes, warthogs, and large herds of Uganda kobs as the sun sets over the African savannah.

Overnight in Queen Elizabeth National Park.
Day 7

Ishasha Tree-Climbing Lions & Kazinga Channel Cruise

Enjoy an early breakfast before heading to the Ishasha sector (if included), famous for its rare tree-climbing lions lounging in fig trees a unique wildlife spectacle in East Africa. In the afternoon, embark on a boat cruise along the Kazinga Channel, a natural waterway connecting Lake George and Lake Edward. This boat safari offers one of Africa's highest concentrations of hippos, massive Nile crocodiles, African elephants drinking at the shore, and abundant birdlife including pelicans and African skimmers.

Overnight in Queen Elizabeth National Park.
Day 8

Transfer to Bwindi & Mountain Gorilla Trekking

After breakfast, drive through scenic highlands and terraced hills to Bwindi Impenetrable National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site renowned for mountain gorilla conservation. Following a morning briefing by Uganda Wildlife Authority rangers, begin your guided gorilla trekking expedition into the dense rainforest. Trekking time varies depending on the gorilla family location, offering an adventurous hike through misty jungle terrain. Upon locating the gorillas, spend one magical hour observing them at close range watching silverbacks display dominance, mothers nurse infants, and juveniles play. This life changing wildlife encounter is often described as Africa's most profound safari experience.

Overnight in Bwindi.
Day 9

Return to Entebbe & Departure

After breakfast, begin the scenic drive back to Entebbe. Stop at the Uganda Equator for photos and souvenir shopping. Enjoy lunch en route before arriving in Entebbe for airport drop off at Entebbe International Airport for your departure flight. End of your unforgettable Uganda gorilla and wildlife safari.

End of safari.
